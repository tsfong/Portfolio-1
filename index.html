About Me

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my page. I'm a 23 year old starting a new chapter of my life, or more like going back and continuing where I left off. I've always been aspiring to be in CS but I took a detour in education. I held onto my interests and combined them in order to become a STEM teacher working for nonprofits and private education companies. I then stepped up and went into administration, where I handled managing one hundred teachers, created my own STEM curriculum, and continued into HR. However, I felt like it was time to pick up where I left off in computer science and decided to leave my comfortable job to pursue my original passion. I love having the freedom to create, design, and implement ideas that come into mind. I'm young, hungry, and 100% ready to take this new role on. I'm here to shape and create the future of my dreams that I put on hold four years ago. The way I see the beauty of coding is verbalized by Fredrick Brooks Jr. who wrote "The programmer, like the poet, works only slightly removed from pure thought-stuff. He builds his castles in the air, from air, creating by exertion of the imagination."
